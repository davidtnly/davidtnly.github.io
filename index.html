About Me

With an eagerness to always learn, I’ve been through roadblocks repeatedly. These have always been the opportunities to adapt and grow. I enjoy this quote by Einstein, “I have no special talent. I am only passionately curious.”

I’ve always had an intellectual curiosity on core principles of psychology such as human cognition and behavior. You can examine the consequences and implications from someone’s actions by breaking it down to the very essence of a pattern or attribute. This led me to Data Science which is an interdisciplinary field created to analytically solve complex problems. By bringing an analytical combination of subjects in Economics, Psychology (behavioral, neuroscience), analytics, statistics, and machine learning, I can bend these subjects to frame new humanistic data-driven questions to drive business decisions.

At loanDepot, I engage in analytics daily to provide a greater visibility into the company's operations by building relationships across departments and understanding the business models, which often includes identifying problems relating to any operating procedures, defining and analyzing project requirements, data visualizations, query creations, and data mining to scratch the surface. It’s an exciting time right now for data analytics. I can be working with Tableau to create live dashboards to attempting to discover new insights using R and statistics to create value and impact.

During my free time I'm always trying to be active by doing activities like hiking, working out at the gym, or taking my shot at some Kaggle data sets. New opportunity? Send an invite!
