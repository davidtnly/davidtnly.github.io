My Story

I’ve always had an intellectual curiosity on core principles of psychology such as human cognition and behavior. You can examine the consequences and implications from someone’s actions by breaking it down to the very essence of a pattern or attribute. In 2015, this led me to Data Science which is an interdisciplinary field created to analytically solve complex problems. By bringing a combination of subjects in Economics, Psychology (behavioral, neuroscience), analytics, statistics, and machine learning, I hope to bend these subjects to frame new humanistic data-driven questions to drive business decisions.

I engage in analytics daily to provide a greater visibility into the company's operations by building relationships across departments and understanding the business models, which often includes identifying problems relating to any operating procedures, defining and analyzing project requirements, data visualizations, query creations, and data mining to scratch the surface. It’s an exciting time right now for data analytics. I can be creating a new ETL process with Alteryx and connect it with Tableau to create live dashboards one day to attempting to discover new insights using programming and statistics to create value and impact the next day.

Some hobbies include trying to be active through hiking, working out at the gym, hitting the bags, maybe do some running, gaming, or practicing my craft in analytics. I might even stream every once in a blue moon on Twitch! (https://www.twitch.tv/skyyyooo)

Feel free to connect with me, but leave me a message and let me know you came through my portfolio!
